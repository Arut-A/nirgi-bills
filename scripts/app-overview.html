Household Bills — Android App

A private utility-bills companion that helps you understand spending and spot deviations.
Version 1.0 · June 2026 · Single device (Galaxy S25) · Built & signed on a home NAS

What it is

A native Android app that puts the home utility-bills dashboard on your phone — and goes beyond a read-only view to help you make decisions: it surfaces what changed, which categories moved away from their normal, and whether your unit rates are drifting. It is installed on one phone only, signs in with your Google account, and shows exactly the same numbers as the web dashboard because both read through one shared calculation engine.

Decision support — understanding deviations

The app's distinguishing feature. For the latest complete month it compares every category against its own trailing six-month baseline and tells you the story, not just the number:

  • What changed — e.g. "May €561 — €136 below your 6-month average of €697 (▼19.5%)."
  • Biggest movers — categories sorted by how far they moved from normal, with the euro delta and percentage, colour-coded by direction.
  • Anomaly flags (⚠) — only genuine spikes are flagged (a category climbing well above its norm); a category dropping to zero out of season, like pellets in summer, is not treated as an alert.
  • Unit-cost trends — each rate shown against its 6-month average, so a tariff drift is obvious at a glance.

Charts & interaction

Readable by design

  • Y-axis value labels (you can read the magnitude, not just the shape)
  • A dashed average reference line on every chart, so deviations are visible against "normal"
  • Smooth animated lines with gradient fills; rounded, animated bars

Interactive

  • Tap any point or bar → a guide line and a value pill (month + exact value)
  • Tap a Costs bar → that month's category breakdown opens below
  • Gaps where data is missing — never a misleading drop to zero

Filtering & analysis

One-tap range presets are always visible on Costs and Usage — no menus to dig through:

12M24MYTD 202620252024All

A range-summary card shows the total, average per month, and peak month for whatever you've selected. The chart legend is tappable to include or exclude categories — the whole view re-slices instantly, on-device.

Security & access

  • Google Sign-In, one account only. The server verifies the Google identity and accepts exactly one allowlisted email — any other account is refused.
  • HTTPS only to the household server; the session token is stored in the Android Keystore (encrypted), never in logs or backups.
  • Fingerprint-ready and single-device by design — no Play Store, no other users.

How the data flows

Utility bill email → Gmail scraper (daily 09:00) → parser → database
    ↓
bills_api ──HTTPS :8443──→ Android app (live on open / pull-to-refresh)
    ↑ shared calculation engine = identical numbers to the web dashboard
  • Freshness: the app pulls live data every time you open it or pull to refresh; offline it shows the last sync with a clear banner.
  • New bills are ingested once a day at 09:00; a Telegram summary is sent on each run, so you are notified when something new arrives.
  • Parity: cost-month attribution and the accrual clip (e.g. annual insurance spread, pellets by heating season) live in one shared module used by both the app and the dashboard.
